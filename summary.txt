Analysis consists of every September 2021 New York City CitiBike trip lasting between one minute and one week, for a total of 3,217,363 individual trips.

Visualizations:
Location of all CitiBike stations
Most popular stations to start and end a trip
Monthly, daily, and hourly usage patterns

The most common start and end points are around the busiest parts of town, while the least commonly used stations are mainly across the river.
Stations are placed at frequent distance intervals throughout the city. Overall trips tended to start in the city center and end more toward the outer edges.
More trips took place on weekdays than weekends. Weekdays had hourly usage peaks near the beginning and end of the work day. On weekends there was a smoother increase to the peak hours in the afternoon.
